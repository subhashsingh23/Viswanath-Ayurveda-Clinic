Spine & Joint Care Treatment

1. Spine Care:

Back pain can be caused by anything, ranging from a minor muscle pull to a significant disc prolapse. Our Panchakarma therapies, Yoga therapies, Ayurvedic medications, herbal combinations, and various exercises are all part of our Ayurvedic spine and joint care program.

The most typical illnesses include:

  • Spinal Slip
  • Sciatica
  • Exploding Disc
  • Osteoarthritis
  • Prolapsed Disc
  • Peri-arthritis with vertigo
  • Spondylitis with Ankylosing

Since the first day of treatment, the results of our pain management sessions have significantly improved. For the maintenance of the spine and joints, we offer many forms of sweda (such as pinda sweda, patrapottali pinda sweda, avagaha sweda, etc.), churna pinda sweda, kashayaseka, kativasti, janubasti, and manyabasti.

2. Joint Care:

  • Inflammation of tendinomus sprains

For the majority of musculoskeletal joint issues and pain, we strive to offer the best relief. We have a knowledgeable and experienced team of experts in joint and spinal treatment.
